# RealCrown Tokenomics

## Total Supply

1,000,000 RC

## Distribution

Liquidity Pool  
Initial liquidity provided for decentralized exchanges.

Ecosystem Growth  
Tokens reserved for platform growth and development.

Community Rewards  
Rewards distributed to users participating in the RealCrown platform.

Development  
Allocated for continued development of the RealCrown ecosystem.

## Utility

RealCrown Token will be used for:

• Rewarding users for activity  
• Converting crown points into crypto  
• Future ecosystem utilities  
• Potential marketplace integrations

## Long Term Vision

The goal of RealCrown Token is to support a decentralized social ecosystem where user engagement directly translates into digital value.
